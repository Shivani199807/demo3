Steel

As one of the largest steel producers in India with a turnover of more than $5.5 billion, JSPL has revolutionised the process of steel making in India. Our ceaseless drive for excellence, unmatched quality and innovative approach have enabled us to become a trailblazer in the steel industry.

Power

indal Power Limited (JPL), a subsidiary of Jindal Steel & Power Limited, has emerged as a significant player in the power industry. Its technical and managerial capabilities have allowed the company to contribute significantly to the growing needs of power in the country and also become the first private player in the country to set up an Independent Power Plant (IPP).

Mining

With the mining capacity of over 9.11 MTPA of coal and iron ore at various locations within India and abroad, JSPL has been able to reduce the dependency on the third party, which has further resulted in a drastic reduction of cost and time consumption. Moreover, owning captive mines has ensured the company of uninterrupted supply of high quality raw material
